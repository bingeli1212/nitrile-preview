---
title: The tabularx-fence
---

The tabularx-fence is designed to typeset a tabular with adjusted
column widthes.


    ```tabularx{fr:1 1, width:5cm, fontsize:footnotesize}
    ---
    & Decimal Value
    & 2's Complement
    ===
    > 0     0b00000000
    > 1     0b00000001
    > 2     0b00000010
    > 126	  0b01111110
    > 127	  0b01111111
    > −128	0b10000000
    > −127	0b10000001
    > −126	0b10000010
    > −2	  0b11111110
    > −1	  0b11111111
    ---
    ```
